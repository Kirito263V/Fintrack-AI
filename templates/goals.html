Goal Lifecycle

From Idea to Achievement

Four simple steps that turn a financial wish into a concrete, trackable reality.

01
Define Your Goal

Name it, set the target amount, and choose your deadline. FinTrack AI handles the math from there.

02
Get a Monthly Plan

The AI calculates exactly how much you need to save per month to reach your goal by your target date.

03
Log Monthly Savings

Each month, log what you actually saved. Watch the progress bar grow and get reassured you're on track.

04
Achieve & Celebrate

When you hit 100%, your goal is marked Achieved. Then set the next one — the momentum is yours to keep.
