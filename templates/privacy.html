Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
Your privacy matters to us. FinTrack AI collects only what's necessary to provide the service. We never sell your personal or financial data to third parties.

1. What We Collect

When you create an account and use FinTrack AI, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: your name, email address, gender, and password hash (never stored in plain text).
  • Income Profile: income type, monthly income, additional income sources, and the number of dependants.
  • Expense Data: categorized monthly expenses you enter (groceries, rent, travel, etc.).
  • Goals: the savings goals you create, including amounts, dates, and progress.
  • Verification Data: OTP tokens used during email verification (expired and cleared after use).

2. How We Use It

We use your data solely to provide and improve the FinTrack AI service:

  • To calculate your savings rate, goal progress, and financial insights.
  • To generate personalized AI-powered tips based on your income and expenses.
  • To authenticate your account securely via OTP verification.
  • To show you your own expense and goal history.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously. Your password is stored as a hash and is never readable — not even by us. All financial data is tied to your unique user account and is not shared between users. OTP tokens expire automatically and are single-use.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties. Your data may only be accessed in the following limited cases:

  • By the secure database infrastructure that powers FinTrack AI (SQLite/hosted database).
  • If required by law enforcement or a valid legal order.

5. Cookies & Sessions

FinTrack AI uses session cookies to keep you logged in. These cookies contain only a session identifier — no financial data is stored in the cookie itself. You can clear cookies at any time to log out.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Update your profile, income, and expense information at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@fintrack.ai.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect the change. Continued use of FinTrack AI constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
