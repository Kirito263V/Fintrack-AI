Our Story

Built for Real Ambitions

FinTrack AI was born from a simple belief — everyone deserves a clear picture of their financial life, without the overwhelm.

Most people know what they earn but have no idea where it actually goes. Budgeting apps are either too complex, too generic, or too manual. We wanted something different — something that thinks with you.

FinTrack AI combines goal-based savings tracking with intelligent expense analysis and AI-powered insights to give you a complete picture of your finances in one place. No spreadsheets, no guesswork.

Why Goal-Based?

Traditional budgets feel restrictive. Goals feel motivating. When you're saving for a vacation, a gadget, or an emergency fund — you have a reason. FinTrack AI connects every rupee you save to something meaningful.
